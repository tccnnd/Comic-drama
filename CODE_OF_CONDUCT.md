# Code Of Conduct

This project follows a practical contributor conduct policy focused on safe,
respectful collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Discuss technical tradeoffs with evidence.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused.
- Credit upstream projects and model providers when using their ideas,
  workflows, or assets.
- Respect licenses, commercial-use restrictions, and attribution requirements.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sharing private assets, credentials, or generated user data without consent.
- Uploading model weights or third-party assets without clear licensing rights.
- Misrepresenting provider capabilities, pricing, or output rights.
- Using the project to bypass platform policies or safety restrictions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may close issues or pull
requests that violate this policy. Severe or repeated violations may result in
blocked participation.
